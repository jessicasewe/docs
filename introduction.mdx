## Intro

Zeepay is Ghana's fastest-growing, wholly Ghanaian-owned fintech company. We focus on building digital rails to connect mobile money wallets, cards, ATMs, bank accounts, and digital tokens to services like international money transfers, payments, subscriptions, airtime top-ups, and refugee payments.

Our mission is to promote financial inclusion, making financial services more accessible and creating a better-connected world.

With Zeepay's API, you can seamlessly send or receive funds across an extensive network, including mobile operators, banks, and agent networks. Securely initiate transactions and tap into our diverse partnerships to ensure smooth and reliable fund transfers.
